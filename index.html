About Us

Our Mission & Teaching Approach

Abeeha Al-Quran Academy was founded with a simple yet profound mission: to make quality Quranic education accessible to every Muslim family, wherever they are in the world.

We believe learning the Quran should be a joyful, spiritual journey — not a stressful chore. Our qualified female instructors create a warm, encouraging atmosphere where students build confidence, correct pronunciation, and a lifelong love for the Book of Allah.

Our Programs

Courses We Offer

Structured programs designed for every age and level — from beginners to advanced memorizers.

📗

Nazra (Quran Reading)

Learn to read the Holy Quran fluently with correct Arabic pronunciation. Ideal for beginners of all ages starting their Quranic journey.

⏱ 30 min/class 👧 All ages
🎯

Tajweed

Master the rules of Tajweed to recite the Quran beautifully and accurately, as it was revealed. Includes Makharij and Sifaat study.

⏱ 45 min/class 📚 Intermediate+
🌟

Hifz (Memorization)

A structured memorization program with daily revision plans, personalized pacing, and regular assessments to help you become a Hafiz/Hafiza.

⏱ 45–60 min 🏆 Advanced
🕌

Islamic Studies

Learn essential Islamic knowledge — Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah, and daily Duas — in an engaging, age-appropriate format for children and adults.

⏱ 30 min/class 👨‍👩‍👧 Families
